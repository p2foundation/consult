Big Name Customers

Constant Capital responds with sound

market judgments and ingenuity to clients' needs for capital. Whether executing an IPO or a debt offering, we integrate our expertise in capital market advisory, transaction structuring and securities placement, Sales and Trading and in Investment Banking to offer clients seamless advice and sophisticated solutions.

We originate, structure and execute public and private placement of a variety of securities: equities, government and corporate debt and related products. Our capital market and capital raising deals include public and private transactions for government and corporate clients across all asset classes and securities. We assist our clients in the issuance of a variety of debt and equity instruments. Be it acquisition financing, growth capital, debt refinancing, distressed asset financing, recapitalisation, pre-IPO placements or shareholder financing, with our extensive knowledge and experience we can identify the right capital, and provide best-in-class deal structuring and execution. to address all our clients’ requirements.


We provide corporate finance advisory services across a range of clients, industries and transactions; we identify, implement and manage strategies that maximize value-creation across all levels of a client’s organization. We assist clients to develop and execute financial strategies aimed at strengthening their market position, gaining access to new markets, sourcing and deploying new capital and restructuring existing businesses for stronger, more efficient performance. We work with a broad range of issuer clients, including private and publicly listed companies, helping to provide comprehensive strategic advice and capital raising expertise. Our services include advising clients on corporate finance transactions, project finance and assisting in the issuance of equity and debt securities for private and public clients.
