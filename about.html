About Us

Introduction

Constant Capital (Ghana)
a registered broker dealer

Constant Capital (Ghana) Limited is a registered broker-dealer and investment advisor, licensed and regulated by the Securities and Exchange Commission (SEC). We are in advanced stages of the licensing process for dealing membership of the Ghana Stock Exchange (GSE) and depository participation (DP) at the Central Securities Depository (CSD). Constant Capital is also a member of the Ghana Securities Industry Association (GSIA).

Constant Capital is a valued partner for many corporates and individual clients and institutional counterparties. We are active in five principal areas: securities trading and brokerage, investment banking, investment research, strategic and financial advisory, and investment advisory services - creative and idea-driven solutions through a robust platform.
We provide our high net-worth and other retail and institutional clients investment advisory and management services and access to a broad menu of investment products and solutions – fixed income and equities brokerage and trading, third-party investment products such as mutual funds, IPOs, saving instruments, and alternative investments. Our research analysts track and analyze in detail, the performance of the economy, markets, securities and different investment vehicles and funds, with a view to helping generate superior returns for our clients.

We are a well-recognized intermediary in the equity and debt capital markets, with strong capabilities in institutional brokerage, investment research, structuring, placements and distribution. Our capital markets team has advised many public and leading corporate clients and successfully executed numerous debt and equity capital raises and strategic advisory assignments.
