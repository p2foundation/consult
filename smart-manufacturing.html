Investment Research

Research is our Core Function

Constant Capital believes that research, fundamental and technical, plays a critical role for investors in selecting quality long-term investments and building diversified portfolios.

Our Investment Research team provides insights and thematic research on the economy, financial markets, industries, and companies. We closely monitor and track changes and spot future trends in economics, foreign exchange, interest rates, equities, and other diverse issues. Our reports provide clients with timely information, valuable insights, and actionable ideas and advice, and constitute an integral component of the product offerings to them.

We keep our clients informed on developments in the financial markets and emerging investment ideas and opportunities, to try to unearth opportunities that offer compelling value for our clients in the markets. Our research products include analyses of monetary and fiscal policy developments and forecasts, up-to-date data, and analyses and commentary on key trends in the interest rate/ fixed income and equity markets., equities insights into selected industries and companies, earnings analysis, etc.

Our full suite of relevant research content includes the following:

  • Economy and Strategy – Economic and GDP reviews, Balance of Payment reports, CPI report MPC preview and reviews, Budget preview and reviews, and Economic and Investment Strategy report.
  • Fixed Income Research - Fixed Income Bond Run, Fixed Income and Currency Market Monitor, Eurobond Market Monitor, Fixed Income Alerts - upcoming pricing (new securities issues), maturities, issuer actions, etc.
  • Equity Research – Equity Market trading reports, Initiating Company Coverage, Earnings Updates and Estimates, company announcement briefs, company valuation reports, as well as insights that are our views on topical economic, industry, and company news.

We seek to recognize risks, opportunities & trend reversals early, and to identify securities which are undervalued, but well positioned for long-term growth.

We believe that our research provides a valuable tool to investors of all types. Our reports are uploaded to our website portal daily, weekly and monthly upon publication

Constant Capital provides relevant and timely content to clients through regular email commentary, weekly publications, and one-on-one meetings.
